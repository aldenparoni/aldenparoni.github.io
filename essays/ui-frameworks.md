Back in my senior year of high school, I took ceramics as an elective just for fun. I had heard that it was a chill class, and it was, but I didn't realize how much time I'd spend in the studio molding my best artistic work. I'm being serious; I still can't believe that the ceramic piece in the picture was crafted through the work of my hands! I'm extremely proud of all the pieces I made in that class thanks to its stress-free environment. The reason I bring this is up is because that was the same feeling I had working with HTML, CSS, and Twitter Bootstrap.
