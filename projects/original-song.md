"Something New" was a song that I wrote on my then-brand new ukulele that was gifted to me on my 18th birthday. The story of how it came about is unbearably cliche; essentially I was crushing hard on a classmate who was already in a relationship, and I wrote the song as a way to cope with my feelings. During the 3rd quarter of my senior year of high school, my audio engineering class was assigned a group project to produce an original song. The class was free to choose their own partners, as long as there were at least two people in the group. I was lucky to pair up with a classmate who I was already clicking with musically.
