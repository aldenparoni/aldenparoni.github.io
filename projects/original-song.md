"Something New" was a song that I wrote on my then-brand new ukulele that was gifted to me on my 18th birthday. The story of how it came about is unbearably cliche; essentially I was crushing hard on a classmate who was already in a relationship, and I wrote the song as a way to cope with my feelings. At the time when my audio engineering teacher assigned the original song project to the class, I had just finished writing the song, not intending to do anything else with it.

I ended up performing the song at our school's annual arts showcase.
